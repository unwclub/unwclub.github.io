Welcome

Welcome to the homepage of United NorthWest Model Railroad Club. We are a Seattle based, non-profit, 100% volunteer group that seeks to better our community and promote the hobby of model railroading. We actively participate in railroad shows and events throughout the year with the club's 3 travelling layouts, as well as annually organizing the regions largest train show where all of the ticket sale proceeds are donated to charity. In the last 3 decades UNW Model Railroad Club has raised nearly $350,000 for charitable organizations in the region with a focus on those helping children and families.

For membership info or any questions, please contact UNW at: join.us@unwclub.com

01-Sept 2023

RE: 2024 WA State Model Train Show & Marketplace at Monroe, WA

UNW Model Railroad Club is excited to announce that dates for the the 31st WA State Model Train Show and Marketplace at Monroe, WA will be February 24th & 25th, 2024!

The Washington State Model Train show is an All-Scale model railroad show featuring layouts and items for sale of all scales, including G scale, 0 scale, S scale, H0 scale, N scale, and Z scale. We have also featured displays of 7-1/2" Gauge live-steam operating trains. This has become the largest All-Scale model train show on the west coast, and continues to be one of the only children's charity fundraising shows in the nation!In the last 3 decades UNW Model Railroad Club has raised nearly $350,000 for charitable organizations in the region with a focus on those helping children and families.
