2024 United NorthWest Model Railraoid Club Presents
The 31st Washington State Train Show &
Market Place, Monroe WA
Feb. 24th & 25th 2024
Benefitting the Snohomish County 4-H Foundation

United NorthWest Model Railroad Club is proud to announce its
31st Washington State Model Train Show & Marketplace
benefiting the Snohomish County 4-H Foundation on
February 24th & 25th, 2024.
The show will be in the same buildings as 2023
at the Evergreen State Fairgrounds in Monroe, WA.

The Washington State Model Train show is an All-Scale model railroad show featuring layouts and items for sale of all scales, including G scale, 0 scale, S scale, H0 scale, N scale, and Z scale. We have also featured displays of 7-1/2" Gauge live-steam operating trains. This has become the largest All-Scale model train show on the west coast, and continues to be one of the only children's charity fundraising shows in the nation! In the last 3 decades UNW Model Railroad Club has raised nearly $350,000 for charitable organizations in the region with a focus on those helping children and families.
